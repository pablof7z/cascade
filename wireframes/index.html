How Thesis Probability Works

Probabilistic, Not Binary Unlike modules which resolve definitively YES/NO, a thesis probability evolves as its supporting modules resolve. The thesis doesn't "resolve" — it reflects ongoing market belief.
Base thesis probability 20%
Fed holds rates >5% (62¢ × +15%) +9.3%
Office vacancy >25% (52¢ × +20%) +10.4%
Regional bank fails (22¢ × +25%) +5.5%
Current thesis probability 34%

As modules resolve, their contribution becomes fixed (either full impact or inverse). Unresolved modules contribute their current price × weight.
