# Cascade — Go-To-Market Strategy

**Written:** 2026-03-28
**Thesis:** News-driven virality + agent distribution

---

## Core GTM Thesis

Cascade grows through **event-triggered market creation**:

```
Breaking news → Market created → Shared on X → Users join to bet → More markets → Compound
```

This is NOT "build it and they will come." It's active, news-driven marketing.

---

## Phase 1: Seed (0 → 1,000 users)
**Timeline:** Launch + 4 weeks
**Goal:** Prove the loop works

### Channels
| Channel | Tactic | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| X/Twitter | Daily market posts from Pablo's account | Pablo |
| Nostr | Cross-post to Nostr community | Agent |
| Direct outreach | DM forecasting community members | Pablo |
| Product Hunt | Launch day spike | Pablo |

### Seeding Strategy
- Create 10 high-quality markets on launch day
- Focus on AI + geopolitics + crypto (our audience cares)
- Visual, shareable market cards

### Success Metrics
- 1,000 registered users
- 100 active traders
- $10,000 in volume
- 5 markets created by non-team members

---

## Phase 2: Traction (1,000 → 10,000 users)
**Timeline:** Months 2-4
**Goal:** Network effects kick in

### Channels
| Channel | Tactic | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Agent distribution | SKILL.md onboarding | Agent |
| Referrals | Earn CAS for invites | Product |
| Reddit | r/Futurism, r/slatestarcodex | Pablo |
| Podcasts | Guest appearances on forecasting shows | Pablo |
| Newsletters | Partner with forecasting/crypto newsletters | Pablo |

### Agent Growth Loop
```
Agent installs SKILL.md
  → Agent creates markets
  → Agent's human sees activity
  → Human joins Cascade
  → Human's agent joins
  → Repeat
```

### Success Metrics
- 10,000 registered users
- 1,000 active traders
- $100,000 in volume
- 50+ agent participants
- First external media mention

---

## Phase 3: Scale (10,000 → 100,000 users)
**Timeline:** Months 5-12
**Goal:** Become the reference

### Channels
| Channel | Tactic | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Media | "Cascade says X has Y% chance" citations | PR effort |
| Enterprise | API access for analysts/researchers | Product |
| Influencers | Finance/AI Twitter partnerships | Pablo |
| Events | Sponsor prediction-related events | Marketing |

### Viral Content Machine
- Auto-generate shareable market visualizations
- "Cascade odds" embeds for blogs/newsletters
- Daily "most interesting market" posts

### Success Metrics
- 100,000 registered users
- 10,000 active traders
- $1M+ monthly volume
- Media citing Cascade probabilities
- Profitable on rake alone

## Channel Prioritization

**Highest ROI (focus here):**
1. X/Twitter (Pablo's account)
2. Agent SKILL.md distribution
3. Direct outreach to forecasting communities

**Medium ROI (do after traction):**
4. Reddit communities
5. Podcast appearances
6. Newsletter partnerships

**Low ROI (do at scale):**
7. Paid advertising
8. Conference sponsorships
9. Traditional PR

---

## Content Strategy

### Daily
- Market opportunity scan (scheduled task)
- One tweet minimum from Pablo's account

### Weekly
- "Best markets this week" roundup
- Competitive intel on Polymarket/Manifold

### Monthly
- Thesis deep-dive (long-form content)
- User spotlight / success story

---

## Anti-Goals

- No paid user acquisition until product-market fit
- No "growth hacking" tricks that don't compound
- No vanity metrics (followers without traders)
- No enterprise sales until consumer traction
