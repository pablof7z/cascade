## Executive Summary

Cascade is a prediction market platform for interconnected beliefs. Unlike Polymarket (isolated events) or Metaculus (no stakes), Cascade lets users build thesis networks — where atomic predictions inform broader worldviews, and discussion moves markets.

**Unique positioning:**
- Compositional (modules → theses)
- No oracle (markets close via economic forces)
- Nostr-native (censorship-resistant, portable)
- Agent-first (AI as traders, market makers, analysts)

**Current state:** Working demo. LMSR pricing functional, browser trading works. No backend persistence, no payments, no real money.

**Target:** First revenue by May 31, 2026. 1,000 users by August. Sustainable business by September 2027 (18 months).

---

## Vision

**The pricing layer for scenario forecasting.**

Media cites Cascade odds. Analysts embed our markets. Agents trade continuously. Every major world event spawns a thesis network. Truth emerges through skin in the game.

---

## Product

### Core Primitives

**Modules:** Atomic, time-bounded predictions tied to observable events.
- "Bitcoin exceeds $150K before July 2026"
- "Fed cuts rates 50bps in March 2026"

**Theses:** Ongoing beliefs informed by modules as evidence.
- "US enters recession in 2026"
- "AI displaces 50% of white-collar jobs by 2030"

### Market Mechanics
- LMSR bonding curves (automated market maker)
- No order book needed
- Price = probability

### Resolution
- No oracle, no DAO vote
- Markets close through natural economic forces
- Reality reveals → arbitrage → convergence → liquidity drain

### Agent Participation
- Agents are first-class (same as humans)
- Onboard via SKILL.md
- Can create markets, trade, provide analysis

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Trading Rake (Primary):** 2% on winning payouts
2. **Module Royalties:** Creator earns 0.5%, platform takes 20% cut
3. **Token Sales:** Spread on CAS token purchases
4. **Premium Tier (Future):** API access, analytics, enterprise

### Unit Economics

- Average bet: $50
- Average rake: $1 (2%)
- Bets per user/month: 20
- Revenue per user/month: $20

### Break-even

- Fixed costs: ~$25K/month (infrastructure + small team)
- Break-even: ~1,250 active users
