# Cascade — LinkedIn Strategy

**Written:** 2026-03-28
**Purpose:** Investor pipeline, enterprise credibility, professional thought leadership

---

## Why LinkedIn

1. **Investors live there** — VCs, angels, family offices scroll LinkedIn daily
2. **Enterprise buyers** — If we want analyst/research clients, they're here
3. **Credibility signal** — Active LinkedIn = "serious founder"
4. **Different audience** — Finance/strategy people who don't use X or Nostr

---

## Account Strategy

### Pablo's Personal Profile
**Primary vehicle.** Investors invest in founders, not companies.

**Profile optimization:**
- Headline: "Building Cascade — Prediction Markets for Interconnected Beliefs"
- About: Founder story + vision + contrarian take on forecasting
- Featured: Investor brief, best Substack essays, product demo
- Banner: Cascade branding

### Cascade Company Page
**Secondary.** For legitimacy and job postings later.

- Company description aligned with investor brief
- Link to product, Substack, socials
- Post company updates (funding, milestones, press)

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Founder Journey (40%)
What investors want to see: you building in public.

- "Here's what we shipped this week"
- "Hard lesson learned about X"
- "Why I'm building this"
- Traction updates (even small wins)

### 2. Market Insight (30%)
Demonstrate domain expertise.

- "Why prediction markets are broken"
- "What Polymarket gets right (and wrong)"
- "The agent economy is coming — here's how to prepare"
- Commentary on forecasting news

### 3. Industry Takes (20%)
Hot takes that spark engagement.

- "Most forecasters are wrong about X"
- Contrarian positions on AI, crypto, markets
- Responses to big news

### 4. Social Proof (10%)
Build credibility through association.

- Podcast appearances
- Media mentions
- Notable users/partners
- Advisor introductions

---

## Posting Cadence

| Day | Content Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Mon | Founder journey / weekly update |
| Wed | Market insight / industry analysis |
| Fri | Industry take or social proof |

**3 posts/week minimum.** Quality > quantity, but consistency matters.

---

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily (10 mins)
- Comment on 3-5 posts from: VCs, prediction market people, AI/crypto founders
- Thoughtful comments, not "Great post!"
- Reply to all comments on own posts

### Weekly
- Send 5 connection requests to: investors, forecasting professionals, crypto founders
- Personalized note: "Building X, saw your work on Y, would love to connect"

### Monthly
- DM 10 warm connections with updates
- Share investor brief with qualified leads

## LinkedIn → Investor Pipeline

```
Post content consistently
  → Build audience of target investors
  → Warm DM with updates
  → Intro call
  → Send deck
  → Follow up
```

**Key:** LinkedIn is top-of-funnel. Move to email/calls quickly.
