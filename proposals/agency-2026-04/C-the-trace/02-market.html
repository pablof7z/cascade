Eight weeks gate-to-launch is the whole trade.

OpenAI has run three internal model gates on GPT-5 in the last six weeks1. Their pattern since GPT-4 is roughly eight weeks from gate to launch. Google's Gemini 2 Pro remains gated behind Vertex AI preview, and the hardware pipeline for the internal TPU cycle slipped in April2.

The compounding evidence is not subtle: Altman's public appearances, DevDay pre-announcement cadence, and the documented two-week window between the final safety eval and shipping3. I put this at 70–72¢. The market agrees — but not enough to close the door.

What would move me? A Google hardware announcement on the week of 21 April with a Gemini 2 Pro date attached4. Without that, OpenAI's lead compounds daily.
