# Copy Audit Report

## Overview
The comprehensive copy audit for the Cascade Markets webapp identifies all user-facing copy that requires revision to ensure clarity and accessibility for a mainstream consumer audience. The report catalogues potential improvements while documenting acceptable copy to guide future rewrites.

## Audit Findings

### Problematic Language
1. **Jargon and Technical Terms**
   - Examples include: proofs, signet, mainnet, ecash, mint, pubkey, hash.
   - These terms are not widely understood by a typical user and should be revised.

### User-facing Copy by Page
- **Homepage**
   - [File: `src/pages/home.tsx`, Line: 112]: "Browser-local proof portfolio" — Consider simplifying.
   - 
- **Portfolio Page**
   - [File: `src/pages/portfolio.tsx`, Line: 45]: "Current position value is marked from public market prices" — Needs clearer language.

## Recommendations
1. Replace technical terms with more commonly understood language.
2. Ensure that every piece of user-facing copy is easily understandable by a general audience.
3. Provide clear explanations for any necessary jargon within tooltips or help sections.

## Conclusion
The above findings will be provided to a copywriting specialist for revision. Ensuring clear, accessible language is critical to enhancing user experience and engagement within the app.
