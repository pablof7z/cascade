How Cascade Works
01

Markets are headlines

Every market is a public claim. The price is the crowd's conviction. Heavy type means strong consensus. Thin type means the world is uncertain. You read the conviction before you read the numbers.

02

Theses link the stories

"If X then Y." Connect markets informationally. Each module informs the thesis but doesn't determine it. The thesis price reflects collective belief in the causal link itself.

03

Markets never close

No oracle. No expiration. Reality asserts itself through price. When information becomes clear, traders act, prices converge, and markets exhaust naturally.
