# Cascade — Investor Brief

---

## The Problem

Prediction markets are the most powerful tool for aggregating information and revealing truth. But they're broken:

1. **Isolated events** — Existing platforms treat predictions as independent. But beliefs are interconnected. "Will the Fed cut rates?" affects "Will housing recover?" affects "Will banks fail?"

2. **Centralized chokepoints** — Polymarket can be shut down. Kalshi needs regulatory approval for every market. Users don't own their data or identity.

3. **No compositional thinking** — You can bet on "X will happen" but not on "If X happens, the world looks like Y."

---

## The Solution

**Cascade: Prediction markets for interconnected beliefs.**

- **Modules** — Atomic predictions (time-bounded, verifiable)
- **Theses** — Belief networks composed of modules as evidence
- **No oracle** — Markets close through economic forces, not trusted third parties
- **Nostr-native** — Censorship-resistant, portable, user-owned identity
- **Agent-first** — AI agents as first-class participants (traders, market makers, analysts)

---

## Why Now

1. **AI agents are here** — For the first time, non-human actors can participate in markets. Cascade is built for them.

2. **Nostr is maturing** — Infrastructure for decentralized identity and data is ready.

3. **Polymarket proved the market** — Mainstream attention on prediction markets during 2024 election. Appetite exists.

4. **Regulatory arbitrage** — Crypto rails + offshore operation = viable path.

## Business Model

**2% rake on winning payouts.** User bets $100, wins $200, we take $4.

At scale:
- 100k users × 20 bets/month × $50 avg = $100M volume/month
- 2% rake on winnings ≈ $1M/month revenue

Break-even: ~1,250 active users
